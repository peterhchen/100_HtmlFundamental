Where is Peter Chen's Family Come From?

Peter is born at Nan-Au Village with given name Hanping. Nan-Au is a small village with 500 aboriginal people.

In 1981-1983, Peter served as second lieutenant in Ma-Chu, a small island of Taiwan frontier.

Peter met 80 years old of Catholic Sister Na from Vermont, USA. Sister Na gives Hanping new name Peter and teaches him Engilsh.

In 1985-1987, Peter studied MSEE and moved to Bay Area after that.

Peter became permanent residences in 1990, and naturalized to USA citizen in 1993.
